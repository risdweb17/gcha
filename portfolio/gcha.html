"Hi I'm Cha,"

a graphic design undergraduate student interested in branding, UI/UX, and web development. Originally from Palm Springs, California, I attended the University of California, Santa Barbara and then the California College of the Arts in San Francisco. I am currently a second year at the Rhode Island School of Design and also occasionally at Brown University. I'm also a designer at Hack@Brown and a project assistant at Concept Link.
I'm interested in creating consumer goods with culture as well as exploring the undiscovered boundaries of digital art.
